The problem

Every conversation starts from zero.

AI assistants are powerful. But they don't know you. Every session is a blank slate — no context, no continuity, no understanding of who you are.

Stateless by design

Every conversation starts fresh. Your AI has no memory of yesterday, last week, or last month. You re-explain yourself every time.

Memory is just notes

Current “memory” features are retrieval hacks — keyword lookups on stored text. They don't understand context, only regurgitate fragments.

Sessions, not relationships

You don't have a relationship with your AI. You have isolated sessions. There's no continuity, no growth, no adaptation.

How it works

Three layers of knowing.

Quarq doesn't store memories — it absorbs patterns. Your preferences, your reasoning style, your communication quirks become part of the model itself.

Step 1

Behavioral Fingerprinting

Quarq observes how you think, decide, and communicate. It builds a continuous behavioral model — not a list of facts, but a deep understanding of your patterns.

Step 2

Weight-Level Adaptation

Your fingerprint is baked directly into the model weights through continuous fine-tuning. This isn't RAG. It's not prompt injection. It's real learning.

Step 3

Personal Runtime

Your Quarq instance runs continuously — always learning, always adapting. It's not an app you open. It's a presence that's always there.
